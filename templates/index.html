Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a vital act that saves millions of lives every year. It helps patients suffering from severe illnesses, accidents, and surgeries.

  • One donation can save up to three lives.
  • Reduces the risk of heart disease.
  • Maintains a healthy blood flow.

How Blood Donation Works

Step 1: Registration

Sign up and fill in your details to check eligibility.

Step 2: Health Screening

A quick health checkup ensures that you're fit to donate.

Step 3: Blood Donation

The donation process takes only 10-15 minutes.

Step 4: Recovery

Enjoy some snacks and rest for a few minutes.

Interesting Facts About Blood Donation

  • Blood cannot be manufactured; it can only be donated.
  • Every 2 seconds, someone in the world needs blood.
  • Donating blood burns about 650 calories per donation.
